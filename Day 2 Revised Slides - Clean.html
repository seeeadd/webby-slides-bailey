Step Two

Context

Example

"I'm creating a new pack of 20 'hunting, fishing, mudding' PNG designs for my Etsy shop. Most buyers are wives shopping for gifts for their husbands who love the outdoors for birthdays, Father's Day, and Christmas..."

Goal
