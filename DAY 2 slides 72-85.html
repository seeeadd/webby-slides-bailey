Why Matching Matters

Most sellers stop at "this keyword has demand."

But knowing WHAT is selling isn't enough.

You need to know WHO is buying.
And WHY they're buying.

That's how you create connection.

That's how you beat everyone else.

SLIDE 81

I call this "The Search Story"

For every product idea, I ask:

WHO is the specific person searching this?

WHAT brought them to Etsy TODAY?

WHY do they need THIS specific product?

WHAT will they actually do with it?
